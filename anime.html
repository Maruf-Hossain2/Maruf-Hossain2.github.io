More About My Anime Preferences:

I tend to gravitate towards anime with strong narratives, memorable characters, and visually appealing animation. I enjoy a blend of different genres, but I have a particular fondness for action, sci-fi, and fantasy themes. I also appreciate anime that explore complex themes and leave a lasting impression.

My favorite anime streaming services include Crunchyroll and Funimation. I am also a big fan of attending anime conventions and connecting with other enthusiasts.
