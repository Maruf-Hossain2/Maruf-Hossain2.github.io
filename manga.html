More About My Manga Preferences:

I enjoy a variety of manga genres, but I have a particular fondness for action, fantasy, and stories with strong character development. I appreciate both long-running series and shorter, self-contained stories. The art style is also important to me; I look for manga with dynamic and expressive art that enhances the storytelling.

I typically read manga online through various platforms or purchase physical copies to add to my collection.
